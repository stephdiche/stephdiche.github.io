Representing Steph’s hometown of Hagonoy, her take on an Absolut Cities poster puts a spotlight on a characteristic arch. The design features a large Singkaban with elements reflecting Hagonoy’s status as a fishing town. Crabs and shrimp represent some of the most loved seafood in the town. Fishermen and nets subtly pay homage to the fishing industry and its workers. Radiating parades festivities, a crab rides, through the arch and under streamers, on a classic Filipino jeepney holding an Absolut Vodka bottle. Lining the bottom are leaves from the plant of the same name. Absolut Hagonoy celebrates the humble town with bright festivities.
