Inspired by the classic two tier cake shape, the new PhynCakes visual identity emphasizes the celebration factor, something PhynCakes values. The colors are inspired by the color aesthetic of typical Filipino fiestas. They are bright and celebratory colors. The orange and yellow combination showing a fun and creative personality while reflecting reliability and quality with blue. The logo utilizes a modified Sans typeface for a simple and readable yet unique look.
