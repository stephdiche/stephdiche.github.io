Come revisit some iconic art movements through Swiss Style posters. Each poster reference one or two artists from Swiss Style and from the subject art movement.
Beginning with Dada Dada, the poster aims to emulate a rhythmic line in Hugo Ball’s Dada Manifesto, the old vintage collage aesthetic, and its anti- philosophies.
Ssssurrealism pays homage to some of Surrealism’s most recognizable artists. Design references Mike Joyce and Alex Grant, both of whom share a “Double Vision” theme.
POP showcases two sides of the Pop Art movement. The American side is reflected in Ben Day dots, comic-style water flourishes, and CMYK color scheme. The British side is painted through a swimming pool scene.
