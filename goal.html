Looking to solve organization problems, the Goal app sets out to meet the planning needs with a goal-oriented process. It targets users working in corporate teams. Interview insights showed goal-oriented planning to be most beneficial to the target user, wherein most users determine the end goal and plan backwards. Goal translates that into a roadmap layout, creating a better collaborative process and organized planning.
