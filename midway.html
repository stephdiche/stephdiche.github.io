Winner of WTF Award at CineMINT Film Festival 2023 Midway tells a story of two beings on a lonely planet realizing that they must meet in the middle to achieve ascension. Portrayed as a soft romance, the colors and textures were inspired by retro sci-fi. The project was an experimentation with 3D animation with Cinema4D.
