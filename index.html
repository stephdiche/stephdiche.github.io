Purpose creates art.


As a storyteller, the essence of the narrative fuels my creations. Written words revolve around my desire to convey a message or a feeling. With multimedia arts, I want to be driven by the same passion. Creativity brings beauty into design while purpose gives it meaning. It is in harnessing both that design produces remarkable solutions and fulfills needs. As I continue my journey eagerly learning and experiencing more, I hope my purpose-driven art will set them apart.
